Insulated Concrete Forms (ICF)

Our ICF system combines the strength of concrete with the insulating properties of polystyrene, creating walls that offer superior thermal performance and structural integrity.

Key Benefits:
  • Up to 70% reduction in energy costs compared to traditional construction
  • Superior sound insulation and comfort
  • Durable and disaster-resistant
  • Fast and efficient installation
  • Environmentally friendly with reduced carbon footprint

Structural Insulated Panels (SIPs)

SIPs provide strong, energy-efficient panels for walls, floors, and roofs. Manufactured under controlled conditions, they deliver consistent quality and exceptional strength. By reducing air leakage and improving insulation, SIPs help lower heating and cooling costs while offering a faster construction process, making them ideal for sustainable building projects.

Key Benefits:
  • Excellent thermal insulation
  • Reduced construction time
  • Eco-friendly material
  • High structural strength
  • Versatile design options for various architectural styles

Expanded Polystyrene (EPS) Roof Insulation

EPS insulation offers lightweight, cost-effective thermal protection for a wide range of roofing systems. Its closed-cell structure provides excellent resistance to moisture and aging, ensuring long-lasting performance. Ideal for both residential and commercial applications, EPS is easy to install and helps maintain indoor comfort throughout the year.

Key Benefits:
  • Lightweight yet strong
  • Affordable and versatile
  • Excellent thermal resistance
  • Moisture-resistant
  • Compatible with various roofing systems and materials

Geofoam Blocks for Infrastructure

Our geofoam blocks provide a lightweight alternative to traditional fill materials in infrastructure projects, reducing settlement and expediting construction.

Key Benefits:
  • Reduces load on soil and foundations
  • Speeds up construction time
  • Lightweight and easy to handle
  • Reliable for large-scale infrastructure
  • Resistant to water absorption and freeze-thaw cycles

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about polystyrene products and their applications in
construction.

Polystyrene provides excellent thermal insulation, significantly reducing energy consumption for heating and cooling buildings. This leads to lower carbon emissions over the building's lifetime. Additionally, our polystyrene products are recyclable and manufactured using environmentally responsible processes that minimize waste and energy usage.

When properly installed and protected, polystyrene building products can last for the lifetime of the building. They resist moisture, mold, and pests, and do not degrade or lose their insulating properties over time. Our products are engineered to withstand the demands of modern construction and are backed by comprehensive warranties.

Our polystyrene products are manufactured with flame retardants and typically used in conjunction with fire-resistant materials like concrete or gypsum board. In applications like ICF (Insulated Concrete Forms), the concrete core provides excellent fire resistance. All our products meet or exceed building code requirements for fire safety.

Polystyrene building systems offer savings in multiple areas: faster construction time reduces labor costs; superior insulation reduces energy costs (typically 30-70% compared to traditional construction); reduced need for specialized equipment; and lower maintenance costs over the building's lifetime. The total cost of ownership is significantly lower compared to conventional building methods.

Yes, we offer extensive customization options for our polystyrene products. We can produce custom shapes, sizes, densities, and performance characteristics to meet specific project requirements. Our technical team works closely with architects, engineers, and builders to develop tailored solutions for unique applications.
